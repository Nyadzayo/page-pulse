PagePulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data does PagePulse collect?

PagePulse stores all data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. No data is sent to any external server. Specifically:

  • Monitor configurations: The URLs and element selectors you choose to monitor, stored locally.
  • Text snapshots: The text content of monitored elements, stored locally for change comparison.
  • Change history: Records of detected changes (old and new text), stored locally.

What data is NOT collected?

  • No browsing history
  • No personal information
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data sent to external servers

Permissions explained

  • Host access (optional, per-site): Requested only when you create a monitor on a specific website. Used solely to fetch the page and check for changes.
  • Alarms: Schedule periodic change checks.
  • Notifications: Alert you when a monitored element changes.
  • Storage: Save your monitors and settings locally.
  • Scripting: Inject the element selection interface when you create a monitor.
  • Offscreen: Parse fetched webpage HTML to extract monitored element content.

Third-party services

If you upgrade to Pro, payment is processed by Stripe via ExtensionPay. PagePulse does not store your payment information. See Stripe's privacy policy.
